# 🎖️ The Resilient Reservist AI Assistant

Welcome to the official AI assistant for **The Resilient Reservist** — a free, always-available chat tool built for military reservists, veterans, and their families. Whether you have questions about the book, need resilience strategies, or just want guidance on navigating military and civilian life, this assistant is here for you 24/7.

---

## 💬 What Can the Assistant Help You With?

This assistant is trained to support you in the following areas:

**The Book**
- What is *The Resilient Reservist: 30 Days to Strength and Stability* about?
- Where can I buy it? (Amazon — available as Kindle, Paperback, and Audible Audiobook)
- Is there a free companion workbook? (Yes — the Family Activity Book, available via rpstrr.com)
- What topics does the 30-day program cover?

**Resilience & Mental Wellbeing**
- Practical strategies for managing stress and anxiety
- Coping with the emotional challenges of deployment and return
- Building mental toughness using Army Master Resilience Training (MRT) principles
- How to maintain a positive mindset during difficult transitions

**Military & Civilian Life**
- Tips for transitioning between active duty and civilian life
- Balancing military obligations with family and career
- Building purpose and identity outside of uniform
- Navigating the reserve lifestyle

**Family & Relationships**
- Strengthening communication with a military spouse or partner
- Resilience activities for children of reservists
- How families can support a service member before, during, and after deployment
- Building a support network at home

**Crisis Support**
If you or someone you know is in crisis, the assistant will always direct you to the **Veterans Crisis Line** — call or text **988, then press 1**. The assistant is not a substitute for professional mental health care, but it can help point you in the right direction.

## 🚀 How to Use the Assistant

Using the assistant is simple — just type your question or concern in plain English, as if you were talking to a knowledgeable friend. You don't need special commands or military jargon.

**Example questions you can ask:**
- *"What is resilience training and how does it work?"*
- *"I just got back from deployment and I'm struggling to readjust — what should I do?"*
- *"How do I talk to my kids about my upcoming deployment?"*
- *"Where can I get the book?"*
- *"Give me a quick tip for managing stress today."*

The assistant responds immediately with a thoughtful, personalized answer. You can keep the conversation going by asking follow-up questions — it remembers the context of your current conversation.

**Quick-start buttons** appear at the top of the chat for the most common questions — just click one to get started instantly without typing anything.

## 🏗️ How It's Built & Hosted

> Think of Claude as a highly knowledgeable assistant who has read the entire book, understands military culture, and is available any time of day or night.

**Hosted on GitHub Pages**
The assistant interface is hosted on **GitHub Pages** — a free, secure, and reliable platform used by millions of developers and organizations worldwide. This means:
- The assistant is always available, with no downtime
- It loads quickly on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop
- No app download or account creation is required
- It is served over a secure HTTPS connection

**Embedded into rpstrr.com via Google Sites**
The assistant is embedded directly into the Resilient Reservist website so you never have to leave the page to use it. It runs inside a secure frame and does not interfere with the rest of the site.

**No data stored — your conversations are private**
Your conversations are not saved, logged, or shared anywhere. Each time you open the assistant, it starts completely fresh. Nothing you type is stored between sessions.
